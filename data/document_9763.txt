The average undergrad student at Truett-McConnell College paid $24,253 based on estimates from 2013. This number represents the overall average net price of all undergraduate students, including those that did not receive any financial aid.
The net out-of-pocket total cost you end up paying or financing though student loans is known as the net price. The reported Truett-McConnell College net price for in-state students $16,010* for the 2013/2014 academic year.

Tuition for Truett McConnell College is $16,650 for the 2014/2015 academic year. This is 34% cheaper than the national average private non-profit four year college tuition of $25,229.
