Diclofenac is a pain reliever in the drug class NSAID (non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drug). It is available both over-the-counter and by prescription in the United States. Its common brand names are Voltaren, Cataflam and Zipsor. Diclofenac is often used to treat pain, tenderness, swelling and stillness resulting from osteoarthritis, rheumatoid arthritis, or ankylosing spondylitis. Short-acting (Cataflam and Zipsor) diclofenac may be used to treat menstrual or other pains. Diclofenac gel or cream is sometimes used to treat actinic keratosis, a skin condition that may become cancerous if not treated
