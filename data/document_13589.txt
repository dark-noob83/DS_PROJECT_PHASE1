Adam Smith was born in Kirkcaldy, Fife Scotland as the son of Adam Smith, a lawyer and civil servant and Margaret Douglas who was widowed two months after Smith’s birth. Smith attended Burgh School of Kirkcaldy from 1729 to 1737, studying Latin, mathematics, history and writing.
