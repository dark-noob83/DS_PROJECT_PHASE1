Hart /hart/ [1 syll.] as a boys' name is pronounced hart. It is of Old English origin, and the meaning of Hart is strong, brave; stag. Surname of an English saint (16th century). Poet Hart Crane; actor Hart Bochner.
Its medieval development as a surname would seem to have been as a nickname, with the bearer having the speedy attributes of a stag. Hart has also from an early time in England and America been Jewish, a convenient anglicized name for German Jewish immigrants. The main spelling variant is Harte.
