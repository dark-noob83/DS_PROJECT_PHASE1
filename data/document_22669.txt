Wisdom teeth removal are frequently done for wisdom tooth tend to have not enough room to erupt in the jaws, being the last tooth to develop and emerge from the mouth (normally between 18 to 24 years of age), which can cause them to be impacted.
The removal of teeth can be performed under local anesthesia, laughing gas (nitrous oxide/oxygen analgesia) I.V. moderate sedation or I.V. general anesthesia. Most commonly, simple tooth removal is performed with local anesthesia only.

General Vs. Surgical removal of wisdom tooth can be done under local anesthesia or general anesthesia. The decision to use either anesthesia depends on the condition of the wisdom tooth and the judgment of the operating dental surgeon.
