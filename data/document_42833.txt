It is of English, Greek and Latin origin, and the meaning of Nora is light; woman of honor. Short form Eleanora (Greek) light, Honora (Latin) woman of honor, and Leonora. Also used as an independent name. In Scotland, Nora is often used as a feminine form of Norman. Nora is the heroine of Ibsen's A Doll's House.
