Temperate Forest. Location and Land. The Temperate Forest is located in many places in both the Northern and Southern Hemispheres. Some places where Temperate Forests can be found are China, Japan, New Zealand, Spain, South Africa, Chile, Marquis, Andes, Alps, and the Himalayas. The continents which include parts of this biome are North America, Australia, Europe, Asia, South America, and Africa. The soil in the Temperate Forest is very rocky, sandy, and is known to be mostly poor quality.
