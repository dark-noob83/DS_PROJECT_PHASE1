Guru Angad Dev Ji: In Sikhism, name of second guru in succession to Guru Nanak Dev Ji. Original name being Bhai Lehna, Nanak made him his successor in preference to Nanak's own sons. Nanak renamed him 'Angad,' ie, one cast in his own mould and filled by his own light, as a veritable part of his own being.
