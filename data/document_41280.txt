Averages for Miami in March. March in Miami is spring time. Many people find spring to be the best time of year to visit Miami, due to the pleasant weather conditions that mean are perfect for days spent lying out on the beach and going for a swim in the sea.
In most years, Miami averages a daily maximum temperature for March that's between 78 and 83 degrees Fahrenheit (25 to 28 degrees Celsius). The minimum temperature usually falls between 62 and 68 °F (17 to 20 °C). The days at Miami warm a little during March.
The average daily temperature in Miami in March is warm at 26 C, which can drop to an average minimum temperature of 18 C. The average temperature of the sea during this month is constant at 24 C. The expectation for rain in Miami in March drops once again, with an expecation of an average rainfall of 64 mm over 7 days throughout the month.
