Cost of Concrete Leveling. In general, concrete leveling costs about half the price of concrete replacement. The cost of concrete leveling is broken down into two components: fixed costs and variable costs. This post is about the cost of concrete leveling for exterior jobs such as sidewalk repair and driveway repair.
