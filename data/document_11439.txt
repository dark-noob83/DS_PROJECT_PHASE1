Tuition. Toledo Law has reduced its in-state tuition from $20,579 to $17,900. Tuition for out-of-state students also will drop – to $29,449. The tuition reduction makes Toledo Law’s in-state tuition the lowest of any law school in Ohio, Michigan, and Indiana. Michigan and Indiana residents pay Ohio in-state tuition rates by virtue of the College’s automatic Michigan and Indiana Resident Scholarship Guarantees. The tuition decrease benefits all students who are enrolled at Toledo Law, whether they are new or continuing, full-time or part-time.
