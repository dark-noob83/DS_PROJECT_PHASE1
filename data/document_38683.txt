Thyroid function test. A blood test measuring your hormone levels is the only accurate way to find out whether there's a problem. The test, called a thyroid function test, looks at levels of thyroid-stimulating hormone (TSH) and thyroxine (T4) in the blood.
The TFT blood test is an abbreviation of the phrase Thyroid Function Test. The TFT blood test therefore refers to any sort of test conducted on a sample of blood with the thyroid gland in mind. There are a few different tests conducted on a sample of blood to establish the condition of this gland.
There are a few different types of TFT blood tests. The TFT blood test may be conducted to check for the functioning of the thyroid stimulating hormone TSH. Another TFT blood test is the T3 blood test where the specific variant of thyroid hormone T3 is tested for. As far as the patient is concerned, the thyroid function test is a simple blood test which is administered in a clinic or in a hospital.
