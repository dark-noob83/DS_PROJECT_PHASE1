The syntax for the PMT function is: PMT(interest_rate, number_payments, PV, FV, Type). interest_rate = interest rate for the loan. number_payments = number of payments for the loan. PV = present value or principal of the loan. FV (optional) = Future value or the loan amount outstanding after all payments have been made.
