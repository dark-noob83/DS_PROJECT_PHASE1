Average Assistant Fire Chief Salaries. The average salary for assistant fire chief jobs is $52,000. Average assistant fire chief salaries can vary greatly due to company, location, industry, experience and benefits.
Assistant fire chiefs in the U.S. earned a maximum average yearly salary of $87,845, according to a 2010 survey of 1,200 cities by the International City/County Management Association. Texas assistant fire chiefs earned median pay of $123,950, while Georgia assistant chiefs made a maximum average yearly wage of $62,719. Salaries for these positions depend on time in office, budgetary allowances and the scope of the position.
Fire Chief Salary. (United States). The majority of Fire Chiefs in the United States — roughly nine in ten — boast more than 10 years of experience in their profession. Salaries stretch from $45K to $120K per year, and the median is $74K annually. Most enjoy medical while more than half get dental coverage.
