The average annual salary for radiologists employed in the United States is $325,438 according to the American Association of Medical Colleges, $373,593 according to Salary.com, $349,000 according to Medscape’s 2013 salary report, and $252,020 according to Payscale. Radiologists are one of the best paid medical specialties, ranking third in Medscape’s 2013 salary survey, after orthopedists and cardiologists.
