Minnesota State University Moorhead is public, 4 year or high located in Moorhead, Minnesota. For academic year 2015-2016, the undergraduate tuition & fees at Minnesota State University Moorhead is $8,092 for in-state and $15,228 for out-of-state students. The costs is estimated by the school. 1 For academic year 2014-2015, the undergraduate tuition & fees is $7,816 for in-state and $14,714 for out-of-state students. For Graduate School, in-state tuition and fees are $6,757 and out-of-state tuition and fees are $12,823.
Undergraduate students pay a per credit tuition rate when enrolled in 1-11 credits. Undergraduate students enrolled in 20 or more credits will have tuition charged using the flat tuition rate plus the per credit tuition rate for each credit over 19.
Tuition for Minnesota State University Moorhead is $6,898 for the 2014/2015 academic year. This is 7% more expensive than the national average public four year college tuition of $6,429. The cost is $12,494 and 64% cheaper than the average Minnesota tuition of $19,392 for 4 year colleges. Tuition ranks 5th in Minnesota amongst 4 year colleges for affordability and is the 63rd most expensive 4 year college in the state. Price does not vary by residence.
