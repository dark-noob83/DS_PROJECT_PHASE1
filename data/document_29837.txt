Etymology & Historical Origin-Talia. Talia is a name with a couple possible origins. It's most commonly considered to be from the Hebrew Talia (טליה) which means ‘dew from God.’ It is also sometimes used as an alternate form of Thalia, one of the nine muses in ancient Greek mythology (she was the Muse of Comedy).
