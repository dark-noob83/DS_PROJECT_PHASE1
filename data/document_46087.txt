To boil crab legs. 1  Half fill a large saucepan with cold water and add a tablespoon of sea salt and seasoning if you wish. 2  Bring the water to the boil and then drop the crab legs in. 3  Reduce the heat slightly and allow the crab legs to simmer and heat up for 5 - 7 minutes.
When cooking frozen crab legs, we have found the best way is to place them in a colander or steamer over rapidly boiling water. The pot should be about one-third of the way filled with water. You will need to cover the pot and steam your frozen crab legs for about ten minutes or until they are completely heated.
