Pacific Time is the Pacific Time Zone of the United States of America (USA) and Canada. Pacific Standard Time (PST) is 8 hours behind Greenwich Mean Time (GMT-8).
In most states in the USA and in most provinces in Canada, Daylight-Saving Time (DST) is observed. During DST PT or PDT is 7 hours behind Greenwich Mean Time (GMT-7). After the Summer months Pacific Time is shifted back by 1 hour to US Pacific Standard Time (PST) or (GMT-8).
