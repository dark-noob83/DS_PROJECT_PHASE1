The cost of tuition and fees at Tulane is $48,305 for the 2014-15 academic year. Room and board add another $12,556. Adding that together with the estimated costs of books and other miscellaneous fees, Tulane expects a total of $63,110 for resident students.
For the 2015-2016 academic year: tuition and fees for a full-time Tulane Law School student are $51,130 ($47,100 tuition and $4,030 in mandatory fees), and the budgeted amount for all other educational expenses that can be covered by financial aid (room, board, books, transportation and other) is $22,304.
