According to the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics the estimated mean annual wage for pharmacists as of May 2008 is $104,260. This would amount to $50.13 per hour. Salary. The median annual salary for a pharmacist was $119,280, or $57.35 per hour, in 2013. The best-paid 10 percent made $147,350, while the lowest-paid made $89,000. The best-compensated pharmacists are employed by merchandise stores.
