Winter Temperatures. Preliminary data indicates that Delaware’s winter temperature was below the 1981-2010 normal for the third time in the last five years. The Statewide mean winter temperature of 34.9°F was 1.4°F below the 30-year normal of 36.3°F. Although slightly cooler than normal, this winter’s temperature was actually near the middle of all winters since records began in 1895 (Figure 1).
