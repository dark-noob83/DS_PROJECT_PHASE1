Driveway resurfacing is an attractive, long-lasting and cost-effective option for renewing an old asphalt driveway. A typical project involves repairing and preparing the old driveway surface and applying a 1 -inch or thicker layer of new hot mix asphalt (or blacktop, the same stuff used on roadways). The cost of driveway resurfacing ranges widely and may start as low as $1 per square foot and go up to over $3 per square foot.

So the cost to resurface a 3,000 square foot driveway would be anywhere from $3,000 to $6,000.
