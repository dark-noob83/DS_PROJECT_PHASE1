Average Temperatures. Average daily temperatures in Port-au-Prince, Haiti's capital, almost always fall between 70 and 80 degrees Fahrenheit. However, record high temperatures have topped 100 degrees, and lows have dipped to 60. At night, temperatures usually fall 10 to 20 degrees from the daily average.
