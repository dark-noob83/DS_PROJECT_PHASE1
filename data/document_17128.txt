The 2014 Huntington Beach, California, population is 191,403. There are 7,156 people per square mile (population density). The median age is 40.1. The US median is 37.2. 51.96% of people in Huntington Beach, California, are married. 11.02% are divorced.
The 2014 Long Beach, California, population is 463,589. There are 9,218 people per square mile (population density). The median age is 33.2. The US median is 37.2. 40.90% of people in Long Beach, California, are married. 10.31% are divorced. The average household size is 2.81 people. 32.48% of people are married, with children. 25.42% have children, but are single.
Long Beach is located in south California. Long Beach is part of Los Angeles County. Long Beach has 50.29 square miles of land area and 1.14 square miles of water area. As of 2010, the total Long Beach population is 462,257, which has grown 0.16% since 2000. The population growth rate is much lower than the state average rate of 9.99% and is much lower than the national average rate of 9.71%.
