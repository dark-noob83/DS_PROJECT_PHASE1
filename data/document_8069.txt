Ratchet & Clank, sometimes referred to as the Ratchet & Clank movie to differentiate it from other parts of the series, is an upcoming CG-Stereoscopic animated action-adventure Sci-Fi film based on the Ratchet & Clank video game franchise.
Ratchet & Clank is an upcoming 3D computer-animated science fiction action comedy movie. It will be directed by Jericca Cleland and Kevin Munroe. The movie is based on the platforming video game series of the same name. The movie was originally scheduled to be released in 2015.
