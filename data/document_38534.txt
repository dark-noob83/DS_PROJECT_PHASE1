Brides and grooms are spending more on weddings than ever before, according to a new survey. The Knot's 2014 Real Weddings survey found that the average wedding spend is now $31,213. And that doesn't even include the cost of the honeymoon. The average in 2013, for comparison, was $29,858. The Knot surveyed 16,000 of their members married between January 1 and December 31, 2014.
