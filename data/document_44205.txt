Not only are dairy foods like milk, cheese, and yogurt excellent sources of protein, but they also contain valuable calcium, and many are fortified with vitamin D. Choose skim or low-fat dairy to keep bones and teeth strong and help prevent osteoporosis. Eggs are one of the least expensive forms of protein.
High protein foods include meat, fish, cheese, tofu, beans, lentils, yogurt, nuts, and seeds. Food sources of protein include meat, dairy products, eggs, beans, nuts and seeds, whole grains, and vegetables. Most foods include some protein, even if only a little.
Good sources of protein Most unrefined foods have some protein in them but mainly meat, dairy products, eggs, beans, whole grains, nuts and seeds. Vegetables have some protein, too.
Fish and seafood. Fish and seafood are good sources of protein and are typically low in fat. While slightly higher in fat than other varieties, salmon packs in heart-healthy omega-3 fatty acids. Try our delicious fish & seafood recipes. Soya.
Food Sources. Protein foods are broken down into parts called amino acids during digestion. The human body needs a number of amino acids in large enough amounts to maintain good health. Amino acids are found in animal sources such as meats, milk, fish, and eggs.
