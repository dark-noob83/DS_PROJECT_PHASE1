Etymology & Historical Origin-Octavio. Octavio is the Spanish form of Octavius, derived from the Latin “octavus” meaning “eighth”. Octavius was an ancient Roman family name mainly restricted to the plebian classes until being elevated to patrician status thanks to Emperor Augustus (born Gaius Octavius).
