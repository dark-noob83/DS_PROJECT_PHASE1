Wedding planners offer hourly rates, flat fees, charge a percentage of a wedding's budget or charge for each service they execute. For example, wedding planners may charge a rate of $50 an hour if they are only working with a couple at certain stages of the wedding-planning process. Or, they may charge a flat fee of $2,000 if they are working with a couple throughout the entire wedding-planning process. Depending on how much a couple uses a wedding planner during the wedding-planning stages, the cost can increase. When you're considering using a wedding planner, think about what you need help with and what you need to make your wedding planning stages more organized.
