Best Answer: There are several possibilities here. #1 The best (and most expensive) is to have a professional moving company do it for you that specializes in grand pianos. Cost upward to $1200 or more. #2 Call some local music stores in the state where the piano is and find out who does grand piano delivery. Check on prices of shipping, but the movers on each end would probably charge a minimum of $200 each. #4 Have someone in the state the piano is in hire the piano movers to get it ready to ship (or drive out yourself and do so).
