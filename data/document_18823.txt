Origin of the name Estefania: Feminine form of Esteban, which is the Spanish cognate of Stephen, which is from the Latin Stephanus, a derivative of the Greek Stephanos, which has its root in stephanos (a crown, a garland, that which adorns the head). From A World of Baby Names by Teresa Norman.
