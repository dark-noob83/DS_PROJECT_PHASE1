Etymology of the name Priscilla. The name Priscilla is the diminutive form of Prisca, and Prisca is the female version of the common Latin surname Pricus, and both are related to names such as that of Priscianus, a linguist during the time of emperor Justinian. The name Prisca has to do with the common Latin prefix prae-and comes from the Latin root priscus, -a, -um, meaning of or belonging to former times.
