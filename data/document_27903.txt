Sherbet is a sweet, icy treat made with fruit juice and milk. Orange sherbet is the classic flavor, but you can make it with any citrus or berry fruit. Sherbet is easy to make if you have an ice cream maker, and the flavor possibilities are endless. Ad.
The ingredients for a durian sherbet are: 1 1/2 cups of frozen  coconut milk, 1 1/2 cups of frozen fresh durian, and 1 tbsp. sugar. 1 person found this useful.
Preparation. Stir together first 3 ingredients in a large bowl, and pour mixture into freezer container of a 5-quart electric freezer. Freeze mixture according to manufacturer's instructions. Pack electric freezer with additional ice and rock salt, and let sherbet stand 1 hour before serving.
