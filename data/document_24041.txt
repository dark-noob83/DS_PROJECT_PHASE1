Definition: The Italian musical term subito, which means “quickly; suddenly,” is used alongside other musical commands to make their effects immediate and abrupt. Examples: 1  subito fortissimo (sff), “suddenly very loud.”. 2  subito forzando / sforzando (sfz), “suddenly with force.”. 3  subito accelerando, “suddenly speed up the tempo .”.
