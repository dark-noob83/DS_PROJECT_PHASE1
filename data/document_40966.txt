Manila, Luzon Climate & Temperature. 1  Manila, Luzon, Philippines is at 14 34'N, 120 58'E, 18 m (59 ft). 2  See map. 3  Manila, Luzon has a tropical wet and dry/ savanna climate (K ppen-Geiger classification: Aw) with a pronounced dry season in the low-sun months, no cold season, wet season is in the high-sun months.
Average temperatures in the Philippines usually range between 21 °C (70 °F) and 32 °C (90 °F) with the average yearly temperature coming in at around 26.6 °C (79.9 °F). Temperatures can fluctuate between regions and depending on the season, however generally January is the coolest month while May is the warmest.
This report describes the typical weather at the Ninoy Aquino International Airport (Manila International Airport/Villamor Air Base) (Metro Manila, Philippines) weather station over the course of an average year. It is based on the historical records from 1974 to 2012.
Manila: Annual Weather Averages. May is the hottest month in Manila with an average temperature of 30°C (85°F) and the coldest is January at 26°C (78°F) with the most daily sunshine hours at 9 in April. The wettest month is August with an average of 140mm of rain.
