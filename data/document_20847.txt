Celebrex is the brand name for the prescription pain reliever celecoxib, the drug's active ingredient. Doctors prescribe Celebrex to treat pain, swelling, and stiffness of arthritis and some other conditions. Celebrex is a non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drug, often referred to simply as an NSAID. NSAIDs block swelling, pain, and fever. Celebrex works by stopping the production of COX-2, a natural substance in the body that causes pain and inflammation. There are two important warnings you should be aware of before taking Celebrex: 1  Celebrex and other NSAIDs can increase the risk of heart attack and stroke that may occur without warning and can be fatal. 2  Celebrex and other NSAIDs may cause ulcers, stomach perforations, and sudden bleeding in your stomach or intestine.
