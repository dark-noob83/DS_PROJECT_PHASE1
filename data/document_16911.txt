The Bureau of Labor Statistics reports that business analysts — sometimes referred to as operations research analysts — earned $78,849, or $37.90 per hour, for 2011. Much like financial analysts, this figure doesn’t account for experience or company size. As of 2012, Robert Half Finance & Accounting estimates that entry-level business analysts earn $44,250 to $57,250, regardless of company size.
