The national average cost for a low-dose screening CT is approximately $200 to $300.This cost – for an asymptomatic patient – is not likely to be covered by conventional insurance. Physicians will need to prepare their patients for the out-of-pocket expense.
Averages. In the end, there is a lot of difference in chest CT scan costs. According to a 2008 study conducted by the American Hospital Directory, average CT scans cost around $250 to $275, with the total charge coming to about $1,750 (the difference being covered by insurance or similar programs). This does not mean every hospital will charge these amounts.
