John Tyler was born on March 29, 1790, in Charles City County, Virginia, to a prominent family. Raised by parents John and Mary Armistead Tyler, he grew up with eight siblings, and they all received the best education available. He studied law at the College of William and Mary, graduating in 1807, at the age of 17.
