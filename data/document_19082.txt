Salary and Qualifications. Executive producers earned average annual salaries of $124,000 as of 2013, according to the job website Simply Hired. This is about 25 percent higher than the average salary the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics reports for a producer -- $92,390 annually. Those in the West region earned between $100,000 and $141,000 -- with the lowest salaries in Montana and highest in California. Executive producers made $97,000 and $196,000 per year, respectively, in Mississippi and the District of Columbia, which represented the lowest and highest salaries in the South region.
Salary by Region. In 2013, average regional salaries varied somewhat for executive producers.
