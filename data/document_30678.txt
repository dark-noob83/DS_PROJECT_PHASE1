When Toronto Has Frosty Weather. Toronto typically has 101 days a year when the minimum temperature is at 0 °C or below. The average date for the last frost in spring is April 13. Frost normally returns here on November 3. The frost-free period extends for 203 days on average between the final spring frost and the first fall frost.
