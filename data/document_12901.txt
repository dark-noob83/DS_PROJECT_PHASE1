The average temperature in Ibiza during summer ranges from 18 to 30°C (64 to 86°F). Summer is also the driest time of year in Ibiza with less than ten days of rain throughout all of the summer months. Winter temperatures in Ibiza drop to between 8 and 19°C (46 to 66°F).
