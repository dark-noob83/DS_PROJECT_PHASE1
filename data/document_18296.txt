Ungulate. Ungulates are the hoofed mammals, belonging to the phylum Chordata. The word ungulate comes from Latin ungula, meaning hoof.. Ungulates are a large group of dissimilar vertebrate animals, grouped together because their outermost toe joints are encased in hooves. There are four ungulate orders. Those ungulates having an odd number of toes belong to the order Perissodactyla.
