Kumon offers math and reading at a monthly cost ranging from $80 to $100 for a single subject, with a beginning registration fee of $50, and material fees of $15. Again, the fees associated are set at the determination of each franchise owner, as are the hours of operation, and will vary.
Let’s face it, cost factors into the equation for most of it. Each Kumon center is independently owned and operated, so costs will vary by location. In general, though, expect to pay $95 – $125 per month, per subject, plus a $30 – $50 one-time registration fee.
Typical recommended program for a K to 12th grade student is in the range of 150 to 200 hours. Typical cost is $45/hour with most centers recommending 6 hours and up per week. … You do the math! SAT/ACT exam prep rate is $65 to $85/hour with recommended programs usually around 40 hours.
