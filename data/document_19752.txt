If you are taking Flexeril and insomnia occurs, be sure to talk to your healthcare provider about possible treatment options. Insomnia is a possible (although rare) side effect of Flexeril ® (cyclobenzaprine hydrochloride). In fact, for the majority of people, Flexeril is much more likely to cause drowsiness.

Cyclobenzaprine is the generic form of Flexeril, a prescription muscle relaxer that relieves pain and discomfort caused by strains, sprains, and other muscle injuries. It is also available under the brand names Amrix, Fexmid, and FusePaq Tabradol.
DOSAGE AND ADMINISTRATION. For most patients, the recommended dose of FLEXERIL is 5 mg three times a day. Based on individual patient response, the dose may be increased to 10 mg three times a day. Use of FLEXERIL for periods longer than two or three weeks is not recommended. (see INDICATIONS AND USAGE).
