Berlin in the Summer. Berlin's summer weather is pleasantly warm, but rarely hot. Temperatures average out at around 20-24°C, and rarely climb higher than 25°C. You can still expect some rainy days, though, so don't visit Berlin without a waterproof jacket or an umbrella. During the summer, the city is alive with events, festivals and shows.
