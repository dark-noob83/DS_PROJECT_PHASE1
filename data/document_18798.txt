The origin of Prairie is the English-American language. The name's meaning is from the flatlands. Prair, Praire, Prairea, Prairee, Prairey, Prairi, and Prairy are variants of Prairie. Prairie is a rarely used baby name for boys. It is not listed in the top 1000 names.
Prairie. Prairies are ecosystems considered part of the temperate grasslands, savannas, and shrublands biome by ecologists, based on similar temperate climates, moderate rainfall, and a composition of grasses, herbs, and shrubs, rather than trees, as the dominant vegetation type.
