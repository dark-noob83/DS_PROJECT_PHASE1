Future Full-Time Tuition and Fees. Tuition for out-of-state students attending Kennesaw State University increased at an average annual rate of 2.6% over the past 5 years. Over that same period, fees grew by 15.0%. Based on this, we estimate the cost of tuition and fees for 2014 - 2015 will total $16,543. At this rate, the annual cost for a two year degree will be $17,239, bringing the total cost of tuition and fees for a two year degree to $33,783.

Annual Total Cost. The total list price for in-state Georgia residents to go to KSU is $18,879 for the 2014/2015 academic year. Out-of-state students who don't possess Georgia residence can expect a one year cost of $28,597. If attending from out-of-state, the tuition is $13,796 which represents a 238% premium. The school charges an additional fees of $1,834 in addition to tuition bringing the total effective in-state tuition to $5,912.
