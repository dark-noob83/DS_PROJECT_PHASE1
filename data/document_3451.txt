Different types of bacon keep for different lengths of time. 1  Cooked bacon in strips lasts for around a week post-cooking, in the fridge. 2  Bacon bits will last for about 6 weeks in the fridge, and 6 months in the freezer. Check the expiry date on the bacon packet. If the use by date has expired, then that bacon is unsafe to use. Always use bacon within seven days of purchase (sell by) or by the use by date listed by the manufacturer. You can also safely thaw and cook the bacon within four months of freezing bacon.
