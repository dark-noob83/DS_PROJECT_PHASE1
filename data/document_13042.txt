The name Minerva is an American baby name. In American the meaning of the name Minerva is: Wise. Latin Meaning: The name Minerva is a Latin baby name. In Latin the meaning of the name Minerva is: Mind; to remember. Minerva was the Roman goddess of wisdom, arts, crafts, and of war.
Origin of the name Minerva: Believed to be derived from the Latin root mens (the mind, thought, intellect). Minerva is the name borne in Roman mythology by the goddess of wisdom, skill and invention. She is equated with the Greek goddess Pallas Athene. From A World of Baby Names by Teresa Norman.
Possibly derived from Latin mens meaning intellect, but more likely of Etruscan origin. Minerva was the Roman goddess of wisdom and war, approximately equivalent to the Greek goddess Athena. It has been used as a given name in the English-speaking world since after the Renaissance.
