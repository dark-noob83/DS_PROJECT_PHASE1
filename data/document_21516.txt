You can determine which version of Internet Explorer is installed on your computer with a few simple steps. To determine the version of Internet Explorer installed on your computer: 1. Open Internet Explorer. 2. Click the Help menu, and then click About Internet Explorer. 3. Next to Version, the first number that appears (example: 5, 6, 7, 8 or 9) indicates the version of Internet Explorer installed on your computer.
