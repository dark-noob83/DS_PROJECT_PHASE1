Microbiologists study the growth and characteristics of microscopic organisms such as viruses. A bachelor’s degree in microbiology or a closely related field is needed for entry-level microbiologist jobs. A Ph.D. is needed to carry out independent research and to work in universities.
Microbiologists typically complete at least a bachelor's degree program in microbiology or a related biological science. In order to enter some fields, such as teaching or advanced research, microbiologists may need to complete an advanced degree program.
