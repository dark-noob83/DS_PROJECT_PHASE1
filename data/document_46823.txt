Undergraduate Tuition. Tuition for Goucher College is $39,808 for the 2014/2015 academic year. This is 58% more expensive than the national average private non-profit four year college tuition of $25,229. The cost is $21,108 and 113% more expensive than the average Maryland tuition of $18,700 for 4 year colleges. Tuition ranks 30th in Maryland amongst 4 year colleges for affordability and is the 6th most expensive 4 year college in the state.
The annual total list price cost to go to Goucher College was $53,538 for the 2014/2015 academic year. The cost is the same for all students regardless of Maryland residence status as there is no in-state discount.
