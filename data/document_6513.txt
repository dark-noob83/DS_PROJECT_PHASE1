HOMEOWNERS INSURANCE EXPENDITURES. The average homeowners insurance premium rose by 5.6 percent in 2012, following a 7.7 percent increase in 2011, according to a February 2015 study by the National Association of Insurance Commissioners. The average renters insurance premium was unchanged in 2012, after rising 1.1 percent the previous year. Florida had the highest average homeowners insurance premium in 2012 ($2,084) and Idaho had the lowest ($538). The countrywide average homeowners insurance premium was $1,034 in 2012.
