Diversity means more than just acknowledging and/or tolerating difference. Diversity is a set of conscious practices that involve: 1  Understanding and appreciating interdependence of humanity, cultures, and the natural environment. 2  Practicing mutual respect for qualities and experiences that are different from our own.
adj. 1. Differing one from another: Members of the same family can have very diverse personalities. 2. a. Made up of distinct characteristics, qualities, or elements: Prague ... offers visitors a series of excursions into a rich and diverse past (Olivier Bernier).
Definition of Diversity. The concept of diversity encompasses acceptance and respect. It means understanding that each individual is unique, and recognizing our individual differences. These can be along. the dimensions of race, ethnicity, gender, sexual orientation, socio-economic status, age, physical abilities, religious beliefs, political beliefs, or other ideologies.
