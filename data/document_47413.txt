Undergraduate Tuition. For students enrolled in a fully online degree program, ASU undergraduate tuition ranges from $490 to $553 per credit hour, regardless of residency status, with no additional program fees. Specific tuition amounts for each program are listed on the individual program pages. For students enrolled in a fully online degree program, most ASU graduate tuition amounts range from $492 to $852 per credit hour, regardless of residency status, with no additional program fees.
