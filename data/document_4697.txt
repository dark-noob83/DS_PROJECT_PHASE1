Tuition and Fees. Tuition at the University of Arizona for out-of-state students is about $31,600 per year ($15,800 per semester). Additional, mandatory university fees equal to around $1,000 per year. More information about mandatory fees and what they entail can be obtained here. *Guaranteed Tuition Policy.
For courses offered at the UA Gadsden Center, tuition rates for 2015-2016 are as follows: In-State: Undergraduate Rate: $339.00 per credit hour. Graduate Rate: $360.00 per credit hour. Out-of-State**: Undergraduate Rate: $932.00 per credit hour. Graduate Rate: $1,018.00 per credit hour. **NOTE: Departmental grants are offered to cover the difference between in-state and out-of-state tuition. Please call 1-888-223-4131 for an application.
