Caprese salad (Italian: Insalata Caprese [insaˈlaːta kaˈpreːze] , meaning Salad of Capri ) is a simple Italian salad, made of sliced fresh mozzarella, tomatoes and basil, seasoned with salt, and olive oil. It was made to resemble the colors of the Italian flag: red, white, and green.
