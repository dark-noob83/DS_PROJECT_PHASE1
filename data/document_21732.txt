There are four rates of pay under the National Minimum Wage (NMW) Act 1998. One for workers aged 16 and 17, one for workers aged 18 to 20 (the development rate), an apprentice rate for those under 19 or who are over 19 and in their first year of apprenticeship, and the main rate which is for workers aged 22 and over.
