Average, Peak and RMS. Audio engineers measure power in terms of average, peak and RMS watts. Peak power is simply the highest reading obtained when measuring the playback of a typical song; it is the most power a speaker can handle in a loud burst of sound. By contrast, average power tells you what a speaker can manage for sustained periods.
