Salary Range. Emergency room nurses earn a median salary of $64,994 per year as of March 2011, according to Salary.com. That translates to an hourly rate of about $31.25 per hour for full-time nurses. The pay rate is very similar to that of a registered nurse working as a typical staff nurse, which is $65,541 per year.
