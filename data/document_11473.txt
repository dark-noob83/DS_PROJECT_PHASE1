Topic 457 - Tuition and Fees Deduction. You may be able to deduct qualified tuition and related expenses that you pay for yourself, your spouse or a dependent as a tuition and fees deduction. To determine whether your expenses qualify, refer to Publication 970, Tax Benefits for Education.
