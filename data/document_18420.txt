Nelson is the transferred use of an English surname meaning “son of Neil”. Neil, in turn, is the anglicized form of the Irish Gaelic masculine name Niall. Nelson developed as a patronymic surname (passed from the father) and is said to mean “champion” (see the name Neil for more information on the Irish-Gaelic origins).
