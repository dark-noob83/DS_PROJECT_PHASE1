Men will probably be happy to hear they can eat more calories than their female counterparts. Because men generally have more muscle and less fat than women, their basal metabolic rate (BMR) - or the number of calories they burn at rest-is higher. So that means men need more calories simply to function each day.
The number of calories the human body burns varies greatly from person to person. At rest, calories burned depend on your genetics, height, weight, age, and sex. Men for example, tend to burn more calories at rest due to a higher percentage of muscle mass which burns more than fat mass at rest.
