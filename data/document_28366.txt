The Gallipoli Campaign (Battle of Gallipoli) was one of the Allies great disasters in World War One. It was carried out between 25th April 1915 and 9th January 1916 on the Gallipoli peninsula in the Ottoman Empire.
The Battle of Gallipoli was a joint operation of British, Australian, New Zealand and French armies; it was the key battle of a campaign mounted to seize Istanbul in order to secure a sea route to Russia. The attack failed through gross incompetence and hubris.
There was no Gallipoli War. People did not enlist in a Gallipoli war: they enlisted in the Australian Army,which sent troops to Gallipoli, one of many campaigns undertaken … during World War 1.
