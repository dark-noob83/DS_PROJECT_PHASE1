Best known as: The Australian actor who played Thor in the movies. Chris Hemsworth is an Australian actor who stormed Hollywood in 2011 as the star of Thor, the big-budget action movie based on the Marvel comic book character. Hemsworth is from Melbourne, and early in his career he spent three years on the Australian soap opera Home and Away (from 2004 through 2007).
Hemsworth is also set to reprise his role as Thor in Thor: Ragnarok, scheduled for a 2017 release, and Avengers: Infinity War Part 1 (2018), and 2 (2019). An Australian rules football fan, Hemsworth was the star of the Australian Football League 's 2014 Everything's Possible campaign.
