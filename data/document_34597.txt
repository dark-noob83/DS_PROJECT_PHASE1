The total list price for in-state Louisiana residents to go to Southeastern is $14,847 for the 2014/2015 academic year. Out-of-state students who don't possess Louisiana residence can expect a one year cost of $27,411.
86% of Southeastern Louisiana University students received grant aid in 2013/2014. The average total aid amount was $6,811. 41 percent of students received aid in the form of Pell Grants from the U.S. Federal Government. The average Pell Grant awarded for 2013/2014 was $4,150.
Tuition for out-of-state students attending Southeastern Louisiana University increased at an average annual rate of 14.1% over the past 5 years. Over that same period, fees grew by 3.0%. Based on this, we estimate the cost of tuition and fees for 2014 - 2015 will total $20,078.
Southeastern Louisiana University tuition is $4,798 per year for in-state residents. This is 25% cheaper than the national average public four year tuition of $6,429. The cost is $6,969 and 59% cheaper than the average Louisiana tuition of $11,767 for 4 year colleges. Tuition ranks 4th in Louisiana amongst 4 year colleges for affordability and is the 27th most expensive 4 year college in the state.
