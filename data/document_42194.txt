Brutus /bru-tus/ [2 sylls.] as a boys' name is of Latin origin, and the meaning of Brutus is dull, stupid. From an old Roman family name, borne most notably by statesman Marcus Junius Brutus, who was one of the assassions of Julius Caesar.
