Inheritance and estate taxes. Tennessee imposes an inheritance tax ranging from 5.5 percent to 9.5 percent on decedents' estates that exceed maximum single exemption limits. For 2014, the exemption amount increases to $2 million. It is $5 million in 2015. In 2016 and future tax years, there is no inheritance tax. Because of the phaseout of the federal estate tax credit, Tennessee's estate tax is not imposed on estates of persons who died in 2005 or later.
