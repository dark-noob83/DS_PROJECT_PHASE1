Salary of an Air Traffic Controller. According to the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics (BLS), the average annual salary of an air traffic controller in May 2013 was $118,650. The average annual salary of an air traffic controller employed by the federal government was $122,490 in the same year (ww.bls.gov). The BLS states that in 2013, the federal government employed 21,090 of the country's 23,060 air traffic controllers
