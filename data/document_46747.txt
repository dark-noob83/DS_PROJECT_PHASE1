The Lehigh Valley's sixth-largest bank, Sovereign Bank, is changing its name to Santander. The bank rolled out the name change Thursday at branch locations in Wyomissing, where the bank was based nationally before moving to Boston in 2011, as well as in Philadelphia, Boston, New York City and Providence, R.I.
Sovereign Bank, N.A., a wholly-owned subsidiary of Santander Holdings USA, is among the 25 largest retail banks in the United States based on deposits, with principal presence in Connecticut, Delaware, Maryland, Massachusetts, New Hampshire, New Jersey, New York, Pennsylvania and Rhode Island.
