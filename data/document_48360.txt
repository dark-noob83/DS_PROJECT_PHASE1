hum. vb, hums, humming or hummed. 1. (intr) to make a low continuous vibrating sound like that of a prolonged m. 2. (Music, other) (intr) (of a person) to sing with the lips closed. 3. (intr) to utter an indistinct sound, as in hesitation; hem.
v. hummed, hum·ming, hums. v. intr. 1. a. To emit a continuous low droning sound like that of the speech sound (m) when prolonged. b. To emit the continuous droning sound of a bee on the wing; buzz.
Full Definition of HUM. intransitive verb. 1. a: to utter a sound like that of the speech sound \m\ prolonged b: to make the natural noise of an insect in motion or a similar sound: drone c: to give forth a low continuous blend of sound. 2. transitive verb. 1. : to sing with the lips closed and without articulation.
