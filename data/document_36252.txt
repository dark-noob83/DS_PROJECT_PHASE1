American Family Insurance just may have scored the biggest coup of the 2014 Super Bowl. Seattle Seahawks quarterback Russell Wilson is starring in a new 60-second commercial from Ogilvy & Mather/Chicago for the Madison, WI.-based insurance company. The 60-second American Family Insurance commercial also features a voiceover narrative by noted singer and actor Harry Belafonte. Enlarge.
