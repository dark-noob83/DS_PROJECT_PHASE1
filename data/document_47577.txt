Symbicort ® (budesonide and formoterol) is a prescription medication used to treat asthma and chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD), including emphysema and chronic bronchitis.
Symbicort contains two different medications: budesonide and formoterol. Formoterol is part of a class of drugs called beta-adrenergic receptor agonists, or beta agonists for short.
The key difference is that symbicort also contains a long acting form of albuterol called formeterol. This long acting medicine has been shown in some studies to increase the risk of asthma related deaths and is only recommended when other forms of treatment are failing. http://www.rxlist.com/flovent-drug.htm.
