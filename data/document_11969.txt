National Averages. In 2012, there were 105,540 occupational therapists working nationwide, according to the U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics. Their average income was $76,400 per year. Eighty percent of occupational therapists earned between $50,500 and $107,070 annually for full-time work. Most occupational therapists have full-time jobs, but approximately 30 percent worked part time as of 2010, reports the BLS. The average hourly wage in 2012 was $36.73.
