Etymology & Historical Origin-Giuliana. Giuliana is a fanciful Italian feminine version of an old Roman name Iulianus (Julius), mainly thought to be the combination of the Italian Giulia (Julia) with the popular suffix “anna”. In the Middle Ages this name was often rendered Gillian (the female version of Julian).
