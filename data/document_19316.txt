Disbursement Costs. Disbursement fees are costs which aren’t covered in the funeral director’s fees. They can significantly vary depending on your local council and whether you opt for a burial in a churchyard or cemetery. The average disbursement costs are between £1,000 and £2,000. The average price of a non-religious funeral is around £3,000.
