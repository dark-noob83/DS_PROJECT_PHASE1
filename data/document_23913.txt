Tuition for University of Hartford is $32,758 for the 2014/2015 academic year. This is 30% more expensive than the national average private non-profit four year college tuition of $25,240. The cost is $10,018 and 44% more expensive than the average Connecticut tuition of $22,740 for 4 year colleges. Tuition ranks 21st in Connecticut amongst 4 year colleges for affordability and is the 10th most expensive 4 year college in the state. Price does not vary by residence.
