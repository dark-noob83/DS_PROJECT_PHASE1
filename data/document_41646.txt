The noun PATHOLOGY has 2 senses: 1. the branch of medical science that studies the causes and nature and effects of diseases. 2. any deviation from a healthy or normal condition. Familiarity information: PATHOLOGY used as a noun is rare. Dictionary entry details.
The adjective PATHOLOGICAL has 3 senses: 1. of or relating to the practice of pathology. 2. caused by or evidencing a mentally disturbed condition. 3. caused by or altered by or manifesting disease or pathology. Familiarity information: PATHOLOGICAL used as an adjective is uncommon.
