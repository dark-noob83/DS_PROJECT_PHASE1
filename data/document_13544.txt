The Goshen branch offers all BMV services. To take a written examination you must arrive at least one hour before the branch closes. You can schedule a driving skills test online, or by contacting the BMV Contact Center at 888-692-6841.
The Nappanee branch offers all BMV services. To take a knowledge test you must arrive at least one hour before the branch closes.
The Bureau of Motor Vehicles (BMV) handles everything to do with driving, like license issuance and renewals, vehicle registration and titling, and license plates.
A: The office of the Bureau of Motor Vehicles in Goshen, Indiana keeps its business hours From Tuesdays to Saturdays. On Tuesdays, they have their timings from 8.30 am to 7 pm. On Tuesdays, Wednesdays, Thursdays and Fridays, the office opens at 8.30 am and stays open till 5 pm. On Saturdays, the BMV is only open for four hours from 8.30 am to 12.30 pm.
