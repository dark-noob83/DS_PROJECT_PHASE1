Corin /cor-in/ [2 sylls.] as a boys' name (also used as girls' name Corin) is pronounced KOR-in. It is of Latin origin, and the meaning of Corin is spear. From Quirinus, possibly derived from the Sabine word quiris meaning spear. Quirinus was an early Sabine and Roman god of war associated with Romulus, one of the legendary co-founders of Rome, who was later identified with Mars.
