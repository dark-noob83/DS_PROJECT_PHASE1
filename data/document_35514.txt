Carb Content. The amount of carbohydrates in kidney beans depends on whether you buy your kidney beans dry or canned. A 1/2-cup serving of canned kidney beans weighs 116 grams and contains 14 grams of carbohydrate. A 1/2-cup serving of dry kidney beans weighs 86 grams and contains 20 grams of carbs. The main type of carbohydrate in kidney beans is starch.
