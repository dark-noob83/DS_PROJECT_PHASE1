New Zealand annual rainfall. New Zealand has a wide range of annual regional rainfalls. Most regions have an annual rainfall of 600–1,600 millimetres per year. Some areas, such as parts of Hawke’s Bay, coastal Canterbury and Central Otago, receive less than this.
