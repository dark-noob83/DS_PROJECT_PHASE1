Plaster City is an unincorporated community in Imperial County in the U.S. state of California. It is located 17 miles (27 km) west of El Centro, at an elevation of 105 feet (32 m). United States Gypsum operates a large gypsum quarry and plant there and owns the town. The 3 ft (914 mm) gauge line runs north to a gypsum quarry and brings gypsum from the quarry to the plant.
