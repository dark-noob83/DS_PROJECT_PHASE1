Typical Costs. The cost for installation of a hardwood floor is usually quoted on a per-square-foot basis. The total cost of a given floor can vary greatly depending on the cost of the flooring material you choose and your location, but you can expect the range to be between $5 and $10 per square foot. Flooring that's in good condition will be easier to refinish than excessively worn or damaged flooring, so expect the labor cost to be between $1 and $3 per square foot depending on the state of your current floor.
