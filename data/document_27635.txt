The entrance sign for Harlington-Straker Film Studios, the front for S.H.A.D.O. Harlington-Straker Film Studios is an England-based motion picture and television production company that serves as the front for, and conceals, the main headquarters of S.H.A.D.O. . It is 40 miles west of Central London. Edward Straker doubles as the Commander of SHADO and the President of the film studio.
