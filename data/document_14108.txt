Loculi. Loculi is a comune in the Province of Nuoro in the Italian region Sardinia, located about 140 km northeast of Cagliari and about 25 km northeast of Nuoro. As of 31 December 2004, it had a population of 538 and an area of 38.2 km². Loculi borders the following municipalities: Galtelli, Galtellì, Irgoli. lula
