University of Mary Washington is public, 4 year or high located in Fredericksburg, Virginia. For academic year 2015-2016, the undergraduate tuition & fees at University of Mary Washington is $11,130 for in-state and $25,294 for out-of-state students. The costs is estimated by the school.
If you register for more than 17 credit hours, you will be charged the full-time rate plus the appropriate per-credit hour rate for each additional hour above the 17 credit hours. See detailed explanation of tuition and fees as well as historical data, 2007 to 2015. Tuition Refund Policy-Fall 2015.
Cost per credit hour (in-state): $395.00. Cost per credit hour (out-of-state): $983.00. Note: These costs are tuition fees only.
University of Mary Washington tuition is $5,190 per year for in-state residents. This is 19% cheaper than the national average public four year tuition of $6,429. The cost is $12,869 and 71% cheaper than the average Virginia tuition of $18,059 for 4 year colleges. Tuition ranks 5th in Virginia amongst 4 year colleges for affordability and is the 70th most expensive 4 year college in the state.
If registered in 15 or more credit hours, tuition for the additional credits (15 or more) will be assessed at a reduced per-credit-hour-rate. The mandatory fees are charged at a per-credit-hour rate for one to 12 credit hours.
