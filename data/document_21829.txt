To open a comprehensive bakery franchise serving bread, treats and sandwiches, opening inventory costs for ingredients and supplies can be as high as $27,000, as of 2015. The Great Harvest Bread Bakery franchise estimates that an average franchisee would pay $17,800.
There are many startup costs associated with opening a bakery. If you are starting from scratch, you need to purchase all of the materials you need to start baking. You also need to ensure you have the tools needed to be successful -- such as mixers, ovens and other basic necessities. New York Magazine describes the startup costs of a recent bakery as totaling almost $40,000. These costs included facility-related expenses such as construction and rental fees, startup inventory and other materials necessary to start the bakery.
