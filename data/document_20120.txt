mortgagee. n. the person or business making a loan that is secured by the real property of the person (mortgagor) who owes him/her/it money. (See: mortgage, mortgagor). MORTGAGEE, estates, contracts. He to whom a mortgage is made.
DEFINITION of 'Mortgagee'. An entity that lends money to a borrower for the purpose of purchasing a piece of real property. By accepting a mortgage on the real property, the lender creates security in the full repayment of the loan in the future. Next Up.
