Morocco's minimum wage, Salaire minimum interprofessionnel garanti, will be raised by 5% in July and another 5% in July 2015, bringing the average monthly minimum salary to Dh2,571, up from Dh2,338 at present. For civil servants, the entire wage increase will be effective as of July 2014. On April 30th Morocco's prime minister, Abdelilah Benkirane, announced a 10% rise in the minimum wage for the private sector, from Dh12.24/hour (US$1.51/hour) to Dh13.46/hour. In the public sector, the monthly minimum wage will increase from Dh2,333 (US$288, at current exchange rates) to Dh3,000.
