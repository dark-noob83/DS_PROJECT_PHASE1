Treating cancer with radiation can cost a lot. Radiation treatment requires complex equipment and the services of many health care professionals. The exact cost of your radiation therapy will depend on the type and number of treatments you need. Most health insurance plans, including Medicare Part B, cover radiation therapy, but you might still have to pay for some part of the treatment. Talk with your doctor’s office staff or the hospital business office about your health plan and how your treatment bills will be paid
