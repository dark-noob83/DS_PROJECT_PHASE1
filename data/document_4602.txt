What is a LPN. LPNs (also known as Licensed Practical Nurse, or Licensed Vocational Nurse) are licensed health care professionals who are trained to handle bedside care of patients dealing with illnesses, injuries and disabilities under supervision of registered nurses, physicians and other health care professionals.
