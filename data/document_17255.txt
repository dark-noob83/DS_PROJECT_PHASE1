The most common medical transcription salary is paid per line of transcription. This can range from $.08 Cents/line to $.15 Cent/line depending on the experience level of the MT, the type of job, and the turn-around time required by the physician. For example, radiology jobs typically have a 4 hour turn-around requirement, but average turn-around requirements are usually in the 24 to 48 hour range. The typical medical transcription salary had median earnings per hour of $15.82 and per year of $32,900 in May 2010.
