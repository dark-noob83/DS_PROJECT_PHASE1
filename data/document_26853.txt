Mi hijo means my son or my child (masc). Mi hijos would mean my children regardless of gender. Typically a term of endearment.
Spanish: Hijo De Puta. Type: Popular Slang. Literal Meaning: son of a whore. Meaning: son of a bitch. Ese hijo de puta me rob a m, minti a mi madre, y abandon a mi hermana. Lo odio. The literal translation would be the son of my uncles. Now, considering the fact that this doesn`t make a whole lot of sense, let me suggest either El hijo de mi tio (the … son of my uncle) or Los hijos de mis tios (the sons of my uncles). One could also say, my cousins or mis cunados.
