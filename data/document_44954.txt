Margarita island: Annual Weather Averages. May is the hottest month in Margarita island with an average temperature of 29°C (84°F) and the coldest is January at 26°C (79°F) with the most daily sunshine hours at 9 in February. The wettest month is August with an average of 121mm of rain. The best month to swim in the sea is in August when the average sea temperature is 28°C (82°F).
