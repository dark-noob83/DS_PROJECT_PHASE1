Even so, this is only a small beachfront community, located 7 km east of Sarina, with about fifty holiday houses, a store and a caravan park. The 3.6 km long beach lies 4 km inside Llewellyn Bay and faces east toward the 5 km wide bay mouth, that extends from Freshwater Point to Allom Point. The beach receives low waves and consists of a low gradient high tide beach, fronted by fine sand to muddy tidal flats that extend up to 2 km off the northern end of the beach. These are relatively safe and usually calm beaches (1148-1149) that can only be used at high tide when the tidal flats are covered.
