The national flag of Armenia, the Armenian Tricolour (Armenian: եռագույն, Yeřaguyn), consists of three horizontal bands of equal width, red on the top, blue in the middle, and orange (also described as colour of apricot) on the bottom. The Armenian Supreme Soviet adopted the current flag on 24 August 1990.
