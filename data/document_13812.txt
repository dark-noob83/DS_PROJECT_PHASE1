Fat. On a weight-loss program you should avoid overeating fatty foods. Limiting fat calories in the diet can also help decrease your overall total calorie intake. Fat should provide approximately 20 to 35 percent of your total daily calories and be mostly in the form of unsaturated fats from plant and fish sources. Foods high in protein also tend to be high in fat. Protein-rich foods should provide 10 to 35 percent of your total daily calories. Adequate protein intake is essential as proteins are the main substances the body uses to build and repair tissues such as muscles, blood, internal organs, hair, skin, nails and bones.
