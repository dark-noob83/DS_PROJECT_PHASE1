Last reviewed on RxList 4/24/2015. Benzonatate is a nonnarcotic antitussive (anti-cough) drug used to relieve cough symptoms. Benzonatate is available in generic form. Common side effects of benzonatate include drowsiness, dizziness, headache, stomach upset, constipation, and stuffy nose. The usual dose of benzonatate for adults and children over 10 years of age is one 100 mg softgel three times daily as required. If necessary, up to 6 softgels daily may be given.
