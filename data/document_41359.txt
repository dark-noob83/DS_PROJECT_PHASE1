ESSEX COUNTY — The average price of a residential property in Essex County is the lowest it has been since 2009, according to new data released by the state's Department of Community Affairs.
Essex, with an overall average price of £280,929 was more expensive than nearby Suffolk (£229,230) and Cambridgeshire (£261,700), but was cheaper than Hertfordshire (£382,543). The most expensive area within Essex was Matching Green (£826,278) and the cheapest was Jaywick (£109,445).
