The average salary for entry level financial analyst jobs in New York, NY is $67,000.
Salary Outlook. The paycheck is good. Financial analysts earned a median of $78,380 in 2013, with the lowest-paid making $48,100 and the highest-paid making $152,420. The best-paid in the profession work in the metropolitan areas of Bridgeport, Connecticut; New York City; and San Francisco.

The average salary for a financial analyst was $91,620 in 2013, which is slightly less than that of financial advisors ($99,920) and mid-range compared with other occupations on our list of the Best Business Jobs. For example, in 2013, a sales manager made $123,150 and a marketing manager made $133,700.
Regional Comparisons. According to the BLS, the states with the highest average annual salary for a financial analyst are New York at $110,450, Connecticut at $106,170, Oregon at $104,370, California at $101,040 and the District of Columbia at $95,340.
