Of this amount, $25,694 was the cost of tuition and $2,847 the cost of fees. The cost of tuition and fees is almost wholly subsidized for residents of Florida. Undergraduate Tuition. Florida State University tuition is $4,640 per year for in-state residents. This is 28% cheaper than the national average public four year tuition of $6,429. The cost is $9,321 and 67% cheaper than the average Florida tuition of $13,961 for 4 year colleges. Tuition ranks 29th in Florida amongst 4 year colleges for affordability and is the 105th most expensive 4 year college in the state. If attending from out-of-state, the tuition is $19,084 which represents a 311% premium. The total list price for in-state Florida residents to go to Florida State is $17,715 for the 2014/2015 academic year. Out-of-state students who don't possess Florida residence can expect a one year cost of $32,159.
