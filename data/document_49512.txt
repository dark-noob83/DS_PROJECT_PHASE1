Prescott is 55 mi (89 km) west-northwest of the State of Arizona's geographic center. According to the United States Census Bureau, the city has a total area of 41.5 sq mi (107.5 km 2), of which 40.7 sq mi (105.4 km 2) is land and 0.81 sq mi (2.1 km 2) is water. Prescott is considered part of North Central Arizona. It is just south of the Granite Dells. The towns of Prescott Valley, 7 miles (11 km) east; Chino Valley, 16 miles (26 km) north; Dewey-Humboldt, 13 miles (21 km) east, and Prescott, together comprise what is locally known as the Quad-City area.
