Overview. Climb aboard one of San Antonio's most beloved family attractions for a nostalgic ride through Brackenridge Park. The miniature railway, The San Antonio Zoo Eagle, travels 2-1/2 miles and crosses the San Antonio River twice. The miniature train is open 365 days a year (weather permitting).
