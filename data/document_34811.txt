In addition to the online-only subscription, you can attend weekly meetings at a cost of between $10 and $15. Members are taught to manage food choices through the point system, says Liz Josefsberg, director of brand advocacy for Weight Watchers. Maybe an apple is zero points, but a candy bar is 10 points.
Before you sign up, consider how much you are willing to spend. Weight Watchers estimates that up to 2 pounds can be lost per week.
