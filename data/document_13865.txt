Despite its alarming appearance, red urine isn't necessarily serious. Red or pink urine may be caused by: 1  Blood. 2  Factors that can cause urinary blood (hematuria) include urinary tract infections, enlarged prostate, cancerous and noncancerous tumors, kidney cysts, long-distance running, and kidney or bladder stones. 3  Foods. 4  Beets, blackberries and rhubarb can turn urine red or pink.
Alternatively, red urine color can be caused by Hematuria. Hematuria, or blood in the urine, will cause a dark or red urine. Blood in urine is a serious matter and may be a symptom of a serious medical issue related to your kidney, prostate or bladder. Seek medical attention immediately if you notice blood in your urine.
