Symptoms of mercury poisoning. Although mercury is poisonous (toxic), it usually only causes problems if you inhale large amounts of it. Very little mercury is absorbed by your body if you swallow a small amount of liquid mercury or get it on your skin for a short time. This is considered almost non-toxic and you'll probably have no symptoms.
