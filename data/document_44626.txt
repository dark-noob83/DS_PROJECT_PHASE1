A workforce management analyst's position includes elements of human resources and operations. On the operations side, a work force management analyst collects metrics on the workforce, analyzes this data to determine what trends exist, and then formulates plans to further the operational goals of the business. Typically, these analysts must have at least a high school diploma with some experience in workforce management or human resources; some companies may require a bachelor's degree in mathematics or another technical discipline.
