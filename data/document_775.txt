Hatfield is a borough in Montgomery County, Pennsylvania, United States. The population was 3,290 at the time of the 2010 census. Hatfield has a city manager form of government with a mayor and a five-member borough council. The mayor is Robert L. Kaler. The borough is part of the United States House of Representatives Pennsylvania District 8 (represented by Rep. Mike Fitzpatrick).
