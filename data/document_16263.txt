Gross salary is what is agreed between you and your employer. Net salary is what you get after statutory and other deductions. To cite an example: Gross salary consists of: Basic Pay, Dearness Allowance, House rent Allowance, Conveyance Allowance, etc. Net Salary is gross salary less the deductions which are made on account of statutory compliances(like PF, ESI, Income Tax, Professional Tax) and other dues (Like Loans and Advances taken) which you owe to the Company and Legal dues if any.
