Marmite (/ˈmɑrmaɪt/ MAR-myt) is the brand name for two similar food spreads: the original British version, since 2000 a Unilever product; and a modified version produced in New Zealand by Sanitarium Health Food Company and distributed in Australia and the Pacific. This is the only product sold as Marmite in Australia and the Pacific, whereas elsewhere in the world the British version predominates.
