Root Canal Procedure Costs. The cost of a root canal procedure may range from $500 to $1,000, and the cost of the post and core procedure to strengthen the tooth is approximately $260 to $360, not including the cost of a crown restoration. The cost for re-treating previous root canal therapy ranges from approximately $695 to $950. The cost for treating a root canal obstruction is approximately $400. The cost of other endodontic procedures, such as an apicoectomy or periradicular surgery, ranges from about $545 to $700.
