Origin of the name Leonard: A borrowing from the Old French, Leonard is from the Old High German Lewenhart, a compound name composed from the elements lewo (lion) and hart (strong, brave, hearty): hence, brave as a lion, lion-hearted.. The name was introduced to England by the Normans but did not become popular until the 19th century.
