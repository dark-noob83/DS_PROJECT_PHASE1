A loin roast will generally weigh somewhere between 2 to 5 lbs (1Kg to 2.25kg). It should cook in a preheated 180 degrees C (350-degree F) oven for 20 minutes per 450 to 500 grams (pound) for a smaller roast, and 30 minutes per half kilo (pound) for a 2.25kg (5-lb.) roast. If cooking a full fresh ham or pork leg, allow 22 to 26 minutes per half kilo (pound).
