A: To pay off a mortgage, a homeowner needs to contact his or her lender and request a payoff letter (sometimes called a demand letter). Frequently, lenders charge a fee for processing the payoff letter request. Some lenders charge a small fee of $5 dollars and others up to $60. In addition to this processing fee lenders also charge a fee to fax the payoff letter to you. Once again the fee can range from $5 to $60. Once you payoff your loan, the release of the mortgage must be filed.
