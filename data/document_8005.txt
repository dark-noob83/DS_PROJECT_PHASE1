The tuition/fees for the one year program is 14,500 EU. Students pay 45% to the institution they attend for the core modules (fall semester) and 55% to the institution they attend for the specialization/these (spring/summer semesters). Students that attend the fall semster at UNCW pay 14,500 EU x .45 = 7,975 EU. Since UNCW requires payment in USD, this amount is converted from EU to USD and payment of $8,482.50 is required for the fall semester and must be paid-in-full by the August drop/add deadline. Students that attend the spring semester at UNCW pay 14,500 EU x .55 = 7,975 EU. Since UNCW requires payment in USD, this amount is converted from EU to USD and payment of $10,367.50 is required for the spring semester (this includes summer thesis registration)
