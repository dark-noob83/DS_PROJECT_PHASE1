Q. How many hours may minors work in a week? A. Sixteen-and 17-year-olds may work up to 44 hours per week. Fourteen-and 15-year-olds may work at most 18 hours per week during the school year and 40 hours per week when school is not in session. Q. What type of work is prohibited for minors? A. Several hazardous types of work are completely off-limits for minors. There are strict limitations on the number of hours a minor may work and on the types of work permissible. In addition, Oregon employers who hire minors must apply for and obtain an annual employment certificate from the Child Labor Unit of the Bureau of Labor and Industries (BOLI), (971) 673-0836.
