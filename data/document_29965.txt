Based on actual events. When the commander of the crew of a B17 Flying Fortress bomber is killed in action in a raid over Sicily in 1943, his replacement, a young naive pilot struggles to be accepted by the plane's already tight-knit Irish American crew.
The Boeing B-17 Flying Fortress and the Consolidated B-24 were the United States' two standard heavy bombers until the arrival of the Boeing B-29 Superfortress in 1944. The B-17 served in almost every theater of World War II, but it used mostly by the US Eighth Air Force, based in the UK, to bombard German targets.

Flying Fortress.  Flying Fortress  is the twenty-fifth episode of the Sonic Underground television series and the first of three episodes in the Chaos Emerald Crisis mini-series. It first aired on 31 March 1999 in France and on 1 October 1999 in the United States. It was included in Volume 2 and Secrets of the Chaos Emerald.
The Boeing B-17 Flying Fortress is a four-engine heavy bomber aircraft developed in the 1930s for the United States Army Air Corps (USAAC).
