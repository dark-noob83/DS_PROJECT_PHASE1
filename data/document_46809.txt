June in Halkidiki, Greece sees favourable weather arrive with the appearance of summer. The average temperature, average highs & lows, average sea temperature and the average sunshine hours all see a rise from May. The average temperature throughout June is 23°C (73°F). Average highs rise to 27°C (81°F) which are mostly experienced in the beginning of the month with the rest of the month seeing daily highs get all the way to 31°C (87°F). Average lows go down to 18°C (64°F) which are mostly seen in the middle of the month.

July is the hottest month in Halkidiki with an average temperature of 26°C (79°F) and the coldest is January at 9°C (48°F) with the most daily sunshine hours at 13 in July. The best month to swim in the sea is in August when the average sea temperature is 25°C (77°F).
