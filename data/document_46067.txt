Undergraduate Tuition. Tuition for Albright College is $37,320 for the 2014/2015 academic year. This is 48% more expensive than the national average private non-profit four year college tuition of $25,224. The cost is $13,212 and 55% more expensive than the average Pennsylvania tuition of $24,108 for 4 year colleges. Tuition ranks 108th in Pennsylvania amongst 4 year colleges for affordability and is the 27th most expensive 4 year college in the state.
