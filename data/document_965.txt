For about 9 years, SUTENT has been a widely used therapy in the treatment of advanced RCC and certain patients with GIST.* SUTENT may help slow the spread of cancer. SUTENT may also help shrink some tumors. SUTENT is not a cure and not all patients will experience the same results.
