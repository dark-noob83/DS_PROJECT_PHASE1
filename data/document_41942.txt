Kindergarten Teacher Salary. Kindergarten Teacher average salary is $35,553, median salary is $33,580 with a salary range from $20,000 to $372,408. Kindergarten Teacher salaries are collected from government agencies and companies. Each salary is associated with a real job position.
Geography Matters. Where you work has an impact on a kindergarten teacher’s starting income. Salaries can vary from district to district and from state to state. According to the NEA, starting salaries for teachers ranged from $26,734 in Montana to $48,101 in New Jersey for the 2011-2012 school year. In Montgomery County, Maryland, the bottom 10 percent of kindergarten teachers earned an average of $38,370 per year in 2013, according to the website Teacher Salary Info. The median salary for kindergarten teachers in the same district was $60,810.
