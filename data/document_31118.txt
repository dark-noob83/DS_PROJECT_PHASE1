Average GPA: 3.86. The average GPA at UC Berkeley is 3.86. This makes UC Berkeley Extremely Competitive for GPAs. (Most schools use a weighted GPA out of 4.0, though some report an unweighted GPA. With a GPA of 3.86, UC Berkeley requires you to be near the top of your class, and well above average. You'll need mostly A's, ideally with several AP or IB classes to help show your preparation at a college level.
