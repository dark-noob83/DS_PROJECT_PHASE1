Kostas /kos-tas/ [2 sylls.] as a name for boys is of Greek and Latin derivation, and the name Kostas means constant, steadfast. Kostas is a variant transcription of Costas (Greek, Latin): version of Constantine. Kostas is also a derivative of Konstantin (Latin). Kreatif forms: Kaestas, Kastas, Kestas.
