Crandall /cran-dall/ [2 sylls.] as a boys' name is of Old English origin, and the meaning of Crandall is  valley of cranes. Place name. Dell is another term for a small valley. Kreatif forms: Caddall, Ciandall, Crandallco. Crandall has 2 variants: Crandal and Crandell. Crandall is a rare first name for men but a very popular surname for both adults and children (#2047 out of 150436, Top 1%). (2000 U.S. Census).
